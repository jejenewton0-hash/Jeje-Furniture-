## Features Implemented

- ✅ **Homepage** — Store name, welcome message, and hero banner image
- ✅ **Product Pages** — 5 furniture products with images, prices, and descriptions
- ✅ **About Us Page** — Store story, mission, values, and why choose us
- ✅ **Contact Us Page** — Email, phone, address, and contact form
- ✅ **Cart Functionality** — Add to cart built into all product pages
- ✅ **Navigation Menu** — Home, Products, About Us, Contact Us
- ✅ **Footer** — Store info, contact details, newsletter signup, copyright

## Challenges

- Setting up and publishing pages correctly on Shopify
- Configuring the navigation menu with the correct page links
- Customizing the theme footer and header to match the store brand
- Understanding how Shopify handles inventory and product visibility

---

## Lessons Learned

- How to use a No-Code platform (Shopify) to build a fully functional e-commerce store
- The importance of publishing pages before linking them in navigation menus
- How to customize a storefront theme without writing code
- How product pages, cart functionality, and checkout work together in Shopify
- The value of good product descriptions and images in e-commerce
